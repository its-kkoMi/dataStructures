Stack Garage

A stack garage follows the LIFO (Last In, First Out) principle. Cars can only be added or removed from the top of the stack. To remove a car that is not at the top, all cars above it must temporarily leave the garage, and after the selected car is removed, the other cars are restored in the reverse order.
